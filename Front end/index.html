FEVER.

Symptoms

  • High body temperature
  • Sweating
  • Chills and shivering
  • Headache
  • Muscle aches
  • Loss of appetite.
  • Irritability.
  • Dehydration
  • General weakness

Medicines

  • Acetaminophen
  • Paracetamole (600mg)
  • Ibuprofen
  • Aspirin

FLU.

Symptoms

  • Fever*/feeling feverish or chills.
  • Cough.
  • Sore throat.
  • Runny or stuffy nose.
  • Muscle or body aches.
  • Headaches.
  • Fatigue (tiredness)

Medicines

  • Oseltamivir as an oral suspension for children.
  • Zanamivir for people 7 years and older
  • Antiviral like Tamiflu or Relenza
  • Mix hot water and vinegar for the best results

FOOD POISONING.

Symptoms [following stomachache]

  • Persistent fever.
  • Persistent nausea or vomiting.
  • Blood in your stools, urine or vomit.
  • Swelling and tenderness to the touch..
  • Jaundice.
  • Muscle or body aches.
  • Pain in any other part of your body.

Medicines

  • Water.
  • Fruit juices with water added.
  • Sports drinks.
  • Broths.
  • Counter medicines such as loperamide
  • Infants should drink breast milk.

EYEFLU.

Symptoms

  • Watery Eyes.
  • Sensitiveness To Light.
  • Discharge From The Eyes..
  • Gritty Sensation.
  • Crusting Of Eyelids.
  • Swelling Of Eyelids.
  • Discomfort While Blinking.

Medicines

  • Chloramphenicol in the form of eye drops.
  • Caused by HSV usually are treated with ganciclovir gel.

SKIN RASHES.

Symptoms

  • Blistering at the areas
  • Itchy and reddene at the ares.
  • Flaky or scaling.
  • Blotchy,burning or stinging.
  • Hive-like (welts),inflamed or swollen.

Medicines

  • Hydrocortisone: Cures skin rashes and itching.
  • Prednisone reduces swelling and allergic-type reactions
  • Diflorasone treats eczema, dermatitis, allergies, rash.
  • Prednicarbate reduces the swelling, itching, and redness
